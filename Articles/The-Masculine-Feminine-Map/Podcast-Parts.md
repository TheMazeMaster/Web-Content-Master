## Podcast Intro

Hi there, welcome to The Steve Day Therapy Podcast.

In this episode, we’re looking at **The Masculine-Feminine Map**.

This article explores a grounded way of understanding masculine and feminine energy without reducing people to rigid roles or stereotypes. It looks at how every person carries both energies, how men and women may experience those energies differently, and how these patterns can shape attraction, conflict, parenting, intimacy, resistance, and repair.

The core idea is simple: this is a map, not a box. Masculine and feminine energies can help you understand how people love, defend, misread, protect, pursue, withdraw, soften, harden, and heal. The aim is not hierarchy. The aim is harmony.
